PROJECT VOID — background ambience
==================================

Drop your track here as:

    ambience.mp3

It will:
  • start the moment the user presses ENTER THE VOID (gesture-allowed autoplay)
  • begin at 70% volume
  • fade linearly to 0% over 30 seconds
  • stop (it does NOT loop)

No file? No problem — the experience runs silently and never errors.

Recommended: a dark cinematic drone / sub-bass pad, ~30s+, -14 LUFS or quieter,
exported as a small MP3 (mono is fine, ~64–128 kbps keeps it light on mobile).
